Privacy Policy

EMI Calculator

Last updated: April 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how EMI Calculator ("we", "our", or "the app") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy. Please read this policy carefully to understand our practices.

1

Information We Collect

We do NOT collect any personal information from users.

EMI Calculator is a fully offline application. It does not require you to create an account, log in, or provide any personal details such as your name, email address, phone number, or financial information.

All calculations performed within the app are done locally on your device and are never transmitted to any server or third party.

2

How the App Works

EMI Calculator allows users to calculate their monthly loan installments by entering loan amount, interest rate, and tenure. These inputs are used solely for on-device calculation and are not stored, shared, or transmitted in any form.

  • All data entered stays on your device only
  • No internet connection is required or used
  • No data is saved after you close the app
  • No background processes or data syncing
3

No Data Sharing

Since we do not collect any data, we do not share, sell, rent, or trade any user information with third parties — not now, not ever.

We do not use any analytics tools, crash reporting services, or advertising networks within this application.

4

No Advertisements

This app currently does not display any advertisements. We do not integrate any advertising SDKs or third-party marketing tools.

This app is completely ad-free.

5

Permissions

EMI Calculator does not request any sensitive device permissions. The app does not access your:

  • Camera or microphone
  • Contacts or call logs
  • Location data
  • Storage or files
  • SMS or phone state
6

Children's Privacy

This application does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Since no data is collected from any user, this app is safe for general audiences.

7

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date at the top. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

8

Disclaimer

The EMI values calculated by this app are for reference and estimation purposes only. Actual loan installments may vary based on your lender's terms, processing fees, and other charges. Always confirm with your bank or financial institution before making any financial decisions.
